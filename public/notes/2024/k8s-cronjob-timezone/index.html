Kubernetes CronJob Timezone

TIL: Kubernetes CronJobs use UTC by default. To use a specific timezone, you need to:

  1. Set the timeZone field in the CronJob spec (requires Kubernetes 1.25+)
  2. Or use timezone-aware cron expressions
apiVersion: batch/v1
kind: CronJob
metadata:
  name: example-cronjob
spec:
  timeZone: "America/New_York"
  schedule: "0 9 * * *"  # 9 AM in specified timezone
  jobTemplate:
    spec:
      template:
        spec:
          containers:
          - name: hello
            image: busybox
            command: ["echo", "Hello World"]

This saved me from debugging why jobs were running at unexpected times in production.
